All day: Arrival of Delegations
Evening: Welcome barbecue at “Vilar Rural de Cardona”

Morning: Opening of Teambuilding

Evening: Eurovillage & Europe’s Night

Sunday the 27th

All day: Teambuilding
Transfer to Barcelona
Evening: Outdoor Movie Night

All day: Committee Work
Evening: Opening Ceremony & appetizers in Gaudinian Gardens

All day: Committee Work

All day: Committee Work
Evening: Theme Party

Morning: Day off in Barcelona

Morning: Opening of the General Assembly

All day: Departures
